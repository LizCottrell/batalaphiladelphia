Batala
Philadelphia

Our mission is to enrich and inspire our communities by providing an Afro-Brazillian musical and cultural experience. We accomplish this goal through arts education and live performances. Our distinct form of musical expression and cultural heritage from Salvador da Bahia, Brazil is open to all individuals regardless of experience or background.
